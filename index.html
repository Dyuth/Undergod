Kaila

The city of Kaila has been ravaged by nightmarish creatures that strengthen their lines with every death.

Survive, plan and submerge in a deep story as you take control of a group of misfits trying to survive in this god forsaken lands.

In this survival RPG you will shape the fate of your party as you face the consequences of every action. Make your choices through each of the four characters' unique personalities and develop them through this unforgiving path.

Find more about the game on our Steam page.
